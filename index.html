About

I am a Master’s student in Physics at National Taiwan University, working on quantum field theory in curved spacetime and analogue Hawking radiation. My current research focuses on particle creation in accelerating-mirror systems and numerical methods for experimentally realistic analogue black-hole trajectories.

Research Interests

  • Analogue Hawking radiation from accelerating plasma mirrors
  • Moving-mirror QFT and particle creation
  • Quantum field theory in curved spacetime
  • Cosmology and large-scale structure

Current Projects

  • Inertial Replacement Method for particle creation
  • Analogue black-hole radiation from laser-driven plasma mirrors
  • Cosmological simulations and baryonic feedback
